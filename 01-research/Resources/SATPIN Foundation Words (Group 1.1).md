## 📋 Progression roadmap

| Week  | Pattern        | Mastery Gate                      |
| ----- | -------------- | --------------------------------- |
| **1** | VC             | Automatic recognition < 2 sec     |
| **2** | CVC core       | Read "sat, pin, sit" fluently     |

| **4** | Blends/plurals | Read 4-phoneme without segmenting |

## 💡 Assessment & Error Matrix

| Error Type | Test Words | Pass Criteria | If Failed → Intervention |
|------------|------------|---------------|-------------------------|
| **Reversals** | in/ni, is/si mixed | 5/6 correct | Letter orientation drills |
| **Final drops** | pant, ants, as | Says all endings | Tap table for final sound |
| **Voicing** | pan/ban pairs | 4/5 correct | Mirror work for /p/ vs /b/ |
| **Blending** | 10 CVC words | < 10 seconds total | Continuous sound practice |
| **Encoding** | Dictate: sat, pin, ant | 4/5 spelled right | Sound boxes practice |

## 🎯 Cantonese L1 Focus

| Issue | Examples | Teaching Strategy |
|-------|----------|------------------|
| **Final /n/ dropping** | pin, pan, tin, an, in | Over-emphasise final sound, use hand signals |
| **/p/ vs /b/ confusion** | pin/bin, pan/ban | Minimal pair practice with mirrors |
| **/t/ unreleased** | tap, pat, pit, at, it | Practice aspiration with paper strips |
| **/s/ position** | is, as, sits, sips | Mirror work for tongue placement |
